Welcome to Wildvision

Wildvision give you the information you need to increase you hunt successes. You will see 2 overlays, weather and animal behaviour. Each overlay works on a traffic light system. For weather, green = huntable conditions, orange = marginal and red = you should earn brownie points. For Animal behaviour green = areas with high animal movements, organge = medium animal movements and red = minimal movement.The animal behaviour overlay is designed to let you know what animals are doing if they are in the area.
